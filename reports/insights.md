# Business Insights Report

## Overview

This dashboard analyses sales data across regions, products and sales channels.

## Key Findings

- Laptop sales produced the highest revenue.
- London and Manchester were strong-performing regions.
- Online sales performed well for higher-value products.
- Retail performed well for accessories such as keyboards and mice.

## Recommendations

- Increase sales campaigns for laptops and monitors.
- Focus marketing on London and Manchester.
- Improve online promotions for accessories.
- Add monthly trend analysis in the next version.
